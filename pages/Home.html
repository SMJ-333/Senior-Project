About Our Museum

Founded in 1892, the Heritage Museum stands as one of the world's premier cultural institutions. Our mission is to preserve, interpret, and share the rich tapestry of human history and artistic achievement.

With over 50,000 artifacts spanning 5,000 years of human civilization, we offer visitors an unparalleled journey through time. From ancient civilizations to contemporary art, our collections tell the story of humanity's greatest achievements.

Featured Exhibitions

Treasures of Ancient Rome

Now through June 2025

Marvel at authentic Roman artifacts including sculptures, mosaics, and everyday objects that reveal life in the Roman Empire. Features items never before displayed to the public.

Modern Masters: 20th Century Art

Permanent Collection

Explore works by Picasso, Van Gogh, and other influential artists who shaped modern art. This exhibition traces the evolution of artistic expression through turbulent times.

Opening April 2025

Discover the sophisticated cultures of pre-Columbian America through artifacts from the Maya, Aztec, and Inca civilizations. Interactive displays bring these cultures to life.
