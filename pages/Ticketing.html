Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

While walk-in tickets are available, we highly recommend booking in advance to guarantee entry, especially during weekends and holidays.

What is your cancellation policy?

Tickets can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours before your visit for a full refund. Within 24 hours, tickets are non-refundable.

Is the museum accessible?

Yes, the museum is fully accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms. Wheelchairs are available free of charge.

Are photography and videography allowed?

Photography for personal use is permitted in permanent collections. Flash photography, tripods, and videography may be restricted in certain areas.
